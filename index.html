Privacy Policy

Overview

Our application ensures user data protection and privacy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

Information We Collect

  • User profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Task and meeting details for productivity management
  • Time tracking data for work hour monitoring
  • Location data for attendance tracking (when enabled)
  • Camera access for QR code scanning and photo capture
  • Usage statistics for app improvement

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app services
  • To facilitate team collaboration and task management
  • To track work hours and productivity
  • To send notifications about tasks and meetings
  • To communicate with users about app updates
  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

Permission Usage

  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes and take pictures for business operations
  • Location: Used for location-based features and attendance tracking
  • Photo Library: Used to select and upload images for business operations
  • Internet: Required for app functionality and data synchronization

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (like Supabase for secure data storage)

Data Security

  • Data is stored securely with Supabase using industry-standard encryption
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Access controls and authentication are implemented
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Row-level security policies protect user data

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. You may request data deletion at any time.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a readable format

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
